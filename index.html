Welcome to Tabletop Magic!

Through this tutorial you will learn how to play Magic the Gathering on Steam's Tabletop Simulator. This is done in four steps: creating a deck, exporting it into a Tabletop Simulator (TTS) file, where and how to save a deck deck depends on whether you're on as Mac or Windows OS, and finally what the controls are for TTS. On top of all that goodness, you will also learn how to add skins to make your gaming experience a lot more interesting!

The first step in the tutorial takes the player to a deck building website such as Tappedout. This site allows players to build their decks' and verify that it is legal for their intended game mode. Then the player will de directed to Frogtown where the newly made decks can be added and all cards are found. This site will then create a Tabletop Simulator (TTS) file that will be used in the next operation. Here the player will have the option to look at the instructions on how to add the TTS Deck file to their Tabletop Simulator game for either Mac or PC. Lastly, the player will be shown all the controls needed to succesfully play a game.
