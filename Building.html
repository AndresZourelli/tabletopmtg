Step 1: Go to Tappedout.net to build your deck!
Click on Deck Builder, which will bring up this menu.
You will have to log in or sign up to be able to add a deck, located in the upper right.
Click on Add Deck to begin!
You don't have to use tappedout to build your deck, you may use mtggoldfish.com, deckstats.net, or anyother deck building website you prefer.

Step 2: Name your deck and pick your format.
Name your new baby deck and choose what format you want it to be. Make sure you choose the same format as whoever you intend to play with.
You must add a description for your deck in order to be able to save it. If you would like other players to understand what your playstyle is you can provide as detailed of a description as you'd like. Personally, I just put whatever inorder to save.

Step 3: Time to add some cards!
Simply type the name of the card you are looking for. Don't worry if you don't fully know the name an autofill option should come up.
To add the card to your deck simply press the "add card to board button."
If you are playing EDH/Commander please mark your commander by writing *CMDR* next to it.
When adding cards make sure they are normal with no added text to it. If you want to add special versions of a card we will do it in a later part. So stay tuned!

Step 4: Overview of Deck and Download of Text File
To obtain the text file version of your deck you will have to click on this buttion which will bring up a small window. Then click on the drop down menu and select the text file option which will automatically download it. This screen gives a nice overview of your deck such as the Mana-curve, how many of each type of cards you have, and if you deck is legal for the game version you are playing.
